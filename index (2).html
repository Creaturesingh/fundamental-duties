Fundamental Duties


Fundamental Duties are mentioned in Article 51-A of the Indian constitution.
Fundamental Duties have been borrowed from the constitution of the eastwhile USSR. It was added through the 42nd constitution amendment 1976.
Initially there were 10 Fundamental Duties. The 11th Fundamental Duty has been added
through the 86th constitutional amedment 2002.
There are at present 11 Fundamental Duties.These are- Abide by the constitution and respect national flag and national anthem.
